## What It Does

Most people click "I Agree" without reading. PROT reads for you.

The extension runs silently in the background as you browse. When it detects a Terms of Service or EULA, it analyses the text against known patterns from consumer protection legislation across Australia, the EU, the UK, and the US ‚Äî and surfaces anything worth your attention.

## Key Features

- **Local analysis** ‚Äî all processing happens in your browser; no contract text is ever sent to a server
- **Multi-region coverage** ‚Äî ACL (AU), CRA (UK), 93/13/EEC (EU), and US consumer law patterns
- **Click-to-scroll** ‚Äî click any finding in the popup to jump directly to that clause on the page
- **Badge notifications** ‚Äî the extension icon shows a count of issues found on the current page
- **Privacy first** ‚Äî no tracking, no ads, no data collection beyond what's needed for your account
